About Electrosphere

Electrosphere is a premier technical platform dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical learning and practical innovation. We empower the next generation of engineers through diverse technical domains.

IoT Innovation

Our Internet of Things (IoT) focus explores the seamless integration of physical devices with the digital world. We build smart systems that collect data and automate environments, solving real-world challenges through connectivity.

Hardware Engineering

From circuit design to PCB prototyping, our hardware projects involve the core of electronics. We emphasize hands-on experience with embedded systems, sensors, and actuators to bring physical products to life.

Software Development

Our software ecosystem focuses on the intelligence behind the machine. We develop robust applications, firmware, and AI/ML algorithms that provide the logic, control, and user interface for modern technological solutions.
